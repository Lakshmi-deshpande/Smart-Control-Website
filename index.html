Four Gestures, Full Control

Your hand, mapped to your machine

Each pose is tracked continuously and translated into a system action — no remotes, no extra clicks.

Move Cursor

Index Finger

Point anywhere on screen and your cursor follows your fingertip in real time.

Play / Pause

Fist

Make a fist to toggle playback on whatever's currently playing.

Adjust Volume

Two Fingers

Hold up two fingers and shift your hand to slide the volume up or down.

Stand By

Open Palm

An open palm pauses tracking, so resting your hand never triggers a stray action.
